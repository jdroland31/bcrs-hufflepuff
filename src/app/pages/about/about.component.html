Team Hufflepuff Hard work, patience, justice and loyalty

We are a group of student developers with a range of experience, working together to create Bob's Computer Repair Shop. This application represents the culmination of everything we have learned in Bellevue University's Web Development program. This cohort has learned and worked together since March 2020 to develop skills in HTML, CSS, Javascript, DevOps, Agile methodology, API development, Express JS, Node JS, Angular and Typescript. Now we are bringing our full stack skills and experience together to deliver this WEB 450 capstone project. You can learn more about our team members below and follow the links to check out their portfolios.
